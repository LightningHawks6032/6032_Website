ABOUT US

Our team, Lightning Hawks is a part of RoboNatick, consisting of three teams, and we are high school students attending Natick High School, Massachusetts. RoboNatick members have an opportunity to select the team once a year. Consisting of 1 senior, 8 juniors, and 9 freshmen, this year, 2019-2020, our goal for the season is to go to the FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) World Championship.
